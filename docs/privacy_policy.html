Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 6, 2026

Inaudite ("the App") is a Reddit Devvit application that helps users discover music shared on r/listentothis by organizing posts into genres and generating YouTube playlists.

Information We Collect

Inaudite is designed to minimize data collection.

  • Public Reddit post information required to display music recommendations.
  • User selections such as chosen genres and playlist generation requests.
  • Technical information necessary to operate the service, such as request logs and error reports.

Inaudite does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information.

How Information Is Used

Information is used solely to:

  • Display music recommendations.
  • Filter tracks by genre.
  • Generate YouTube playlist links.
  • Maintain and improve the App.
  • Diagnose technical issues.

Third-Party Services

Inaudite may interact with third-party services including Reddit, YouTube, and any hosting or infrastructure providers required to operate the App. Use of those services is governed by their own privacy policies.

Data Storage

Inaudite aims to store as little user information as possible. Operational logs may be retained temporarily for debugging, security, and service reliability.

Cookies

Inaudite does not use cookies directly. Any cookies are managed by Reddit or other third-party services you interact with.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age required by Reddit's Terms of Service.
